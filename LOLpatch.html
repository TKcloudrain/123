League of Legends Patch 13.18: Major Balance Adjustments and New Features

Champion Balance Changes:

Patch 13.18 brought notable adjustments to several champions, including major changes to Akali and Jhin. Akali, a versatile assassin, saw a reduction in her Q ability damage and increased cooldowns on her ultimate. These changes were aimed at reducing her burst damage potential and making her less dominant in high-stakes situations.

Jhin, on the other hand, experienced adjustments to his passive and ultimate abilities. The patch decreased his base attack damage and reduced the crit chance on his fourth shot. These changes were implemented to balance his high burst potential and mitigate his overpowering presence in the late game. Riot Games aimed to make Jhin less of a dominant force and more balanced across various stages of the game.

Item Changes:

Several key items also received updates in Patch 13.18. The Infinity Edge item was adjusted to provide less critical strike chance but increased its damage output on critical hits. This change aimed to balance the item’s impact and ensure it did not overshadow other damage items in terms of overall effectiveness.

Another significant change was the adjustment to the Riftmaker item, which saw a reduction in its omnivamp effect but an increase in its ability power scaling. This change aimed to balance the item’s impact on sustain and damage, making it a more strategic choice for mages.

New Features:

Patch 13.18 also introduced new features, including the addition of the "Champion Mastery" system improvements. Players now have more detailed statistics on their performance with individual champions, including win rates and play styles. This addition allows players to better understand their strengths and weaknesses and make informed decisions about champion selection and playstyle.

Community Reaction:

The community's response to Patch 13.18 has been mixed. Professional players and analysts have noted that the changes have led to a more balanced meta, but some players feel that the adjustments have made certain champions less enjoyable to play. The new Champion Mastery system has generally been well-received, with many players appreciating the added depth of performance tracking.
