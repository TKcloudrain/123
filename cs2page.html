Huge CS2 Map Update

de_mills:

This map immerses players in a chaotic industrial setting featuring old factories and manufacturing facilities. The map's layout is complex, with numerous obstacles and industrial equipment creating opportunities for ambushes and close-quarters combat. High platforms and various cover points provide interesting tactical options for both attacking and defending teams.

de_thera:

de_thera offers a mysterious atmosphere set in an ancient temple or archaeological site. The map features a complex structure with large open spaces, narrow corridors, and grand halls, creating ideal conditions for strategic planning. Elevated platforms and underground tunnels add a vertical element to gameplay, allowing players to employ a variety of tactics and strategies.

de_assembly:

This map unfolds in a modern high-tech center or research facility. Its design includes clean lines, spacious interiors, and vast open areas, offering a wide range of tactical possibilities. The large open spaces and technical structures allow players to plan attacks and defenses on different levels, using both cover and open areas for various playstyles.

de_memento:

de_memento evokes a nostalgic feel, set in an old urban district with characteristic architecture and narrow streets. The map's layout includes multiple levels, enabling players to utilize both open spaces for swift attacks and enclosed areas for ambushes. The balanced placement of cover and open areas provides diverse tactical options.

fy_pool_day:

fy_poolday presents a light-hearted, relaxed setting by a pool, perfect for less serious matches. The map features beach umbrellas, loungers, and water elements, creating a fun and informal atmosphere. With its small size and compact layout, the map facilitates fast-paced, dynamic gameplay with unconventional tactical solutions.
