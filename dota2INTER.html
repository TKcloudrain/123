Dota 2

"The International 2024" Main Event Starts

The main event of The International 2024 began on October 12, 2024, at the Singapore Indoor Stadium, marking the climax of the Dota 2 competitive season. This prestigious tournament brings together the best teams from around the globe to compete for the title of Aegis of Champions.

Tournament Format:

The main event of The International 2024 runs from October 12 to October 29, 2024. The tournament features several stages, including group stages, playoffs, and the grand finals. Teams that have qualified through the regional qualifiers will compete in a series of high-stakes matches to determine the ultimate champion.

Stakes and Expectations:

The International is not only about the massive prize pool, which exceeds $40 million, but also about the honor and prestige of being crowned the world champion. Teams are not only competing for financial rewards but also for a place in the annals of Dota 2 history. The intense competition and high level of play ensure that every match is thrilling and closely watched by fans.

Fan Engagement:

Fans around the world eagerly follow the tournament, with live viewership and community engagement reaching new heights. The excitement surrounding the event contributes to the overall atmosphere, making The International 2024 a memorable experience for both players and spectators.
